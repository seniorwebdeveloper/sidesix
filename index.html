Design, The Web, & Everything

My philosophy about design, the web, and everything is: stuff should just work .

I shouldn't have to flick all over the page when I'm using my phone.

The web should respond to what I'm doing, where I'm doing it, and the device I'm doing it on. For example if I'm surfing the web on my iPhone in a starbucks, the web pages should be designed for an iPhone using a fairly slow connection.

Craftmanship is crucial.

I watched my Great-Aunt Ethel fire eight different guys who wanted to build an addition onto her house. She kept saying that "they didn't know what they were doing." She meant they had no sense of quality; that they were not craftsmen and they had no business working on her home.

"If I had asked people what they wanted, they would have said a faster horse."
— Henry Ford
